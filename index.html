About Me

I am a Front-end Web Developer at the National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL) in Golden, CO, managing 26 NREL.gov subsites, 36 intranet subsites, and two U.S. Department of Energy websites. Previously, I was a Web Editor for Leidos, supporting the National Science Foundation's U.S. Antarctic Program (USAP), including deploying a new intranet at South Pole Station.

My technical expertise includes HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 4/5, XML, JavaScript, Reactstrap, Node.js, MySQL, and Git. I work primarily in Visual Studio Code and am proficient in OU Campus CMS, ColdFusion CMS, Adobe Photoshop, and Dreamweaver. My background also includes print design with Adobe InDesign.

I hold a B.S. in English - Professional Writing with an HCI/UX minor from the University of North Alabama, an A.A.S. in Business Administration - Paralegal Studies from Calhoun Community College, and a certificate from the University of Denver's Coding Boot Camp.

Currently, I am pursuing an M.A. in Art and Technology at the University of Oklahoma (expected Summer 2025), focusing on object-oriented programming, computational media, and interactive design.
